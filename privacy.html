AutoMate AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 February 2026

1. What We Collect

AutoMate AI ("the App") collects the minimum data needed to provide vehicle tracking services:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you sign in via email, Google, or Apple.
  • Vehicle data: Registration numbers you enter, plus MOT, tax, and insurance dates retrieved from DVLA/DVSA public APIs on your behalf.
  • Device tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens for sending reminder notifications.
  • Preferences: Notification settings and premium subscription status, stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your vehicles' compliance status (MOT, tax, insurance).
  • Send local and push notification reminders for upcoming deadlines.
  • Authenticate your identity for account access.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Messaging, Crashlytics. See Firebase Privacy.
  • DVLA/DVSA APIs: Vehicle and MOT data is retrieved from UK government APIs. No personal data is sent — only registration numbers.
  • Apple/Google Sign-In: Handled by platform SDKs; we receive only the identity token and basic profile.

4. Data Storage and Security

Account and vehicle data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, protected by Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules. Local preferences are stored on-device via SharedPreferences. API keys are compiled into the app binary and are not stored on our servers.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen in the app. We will delete all your Firestore data within 30 days of account deletion.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below or use the in-app account deletion feature.

7. Children

The App is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@automate-ai.app

Powered by official DVLA data sources and built for UK compliance.
