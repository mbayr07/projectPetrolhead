Reliable Answers

Everything you need to know.

Is AutoMate AI free?

Yes. Instant DVLA checks and basic reminders for up to 2 vehicles are free forever. Upgrade for unlimited vehicles and advanced reminders.

Do I need an account?

No. Quick checks work without login. An account unlocks garage management and reminders.

How much data do I need to share to check a vehicle?

Just the registration number. Basic checks do not require an account or any personal details.

Where is my data stored?

Minimal data is collected (email and vehicle data). Documents are stored locally on device for privacy.

Are you affiliated with DVLA?

No. AutoMate AI uses publicly available DVLA APIs but is not affiliated with DVLA.
